PUP Virtual Job Fair Portal

Project Name: PUP Virtual Job Fair Portal

Tech Stack Used: Figma, HTML, CSS, Laravel

This project is for our Software Engineering subject at PUP(). I was assigned to create the wireframes and prototype of the system using Figma. I'm also a part of the documentation and front-end development team.

Project Goals

The PUP Job Fair Portal strives to offer the Students and Alumni of Polytechnic University of the Philippines, to find their future job with an interactive virtual job fair, in spite of the pandemic. The PUP Job Fair Portal seeks to assist the matching and communication of employment opportunities between job seekers and companies.

PUP Job Fair Portal aims to continuously improve the value of service for both job seekers and employers. To that aim, the team is continually developing its product and service offerings in order to facilitate the connection between the job seekers and the employers for the job fair portal.
